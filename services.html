Our coaching services

Detailed breakdown of what we offer — the same programmes you’ll see summarised on our homepage. Not sure which fits? Message us and we’ll recommend the best format.

1-to-1 football coaching

One coach, one player: maximum touches, instant feedback, and a session plan written around your child’s strengths and gaps. Ideal when you want fast progress on technique, confidence, or a specific part of the game.

  • Ball mastery, first touch and passing under pressure
  • Finishing, 1v1 attacking moves and defending fundamentals
  • Match scenarios so skills transfer to Saturday or Sunday league
  • Clear targets each session so players see their own improvement

Kids football training (ages 6–15+)

Young players learn best when sessions feel like play with a purpose. We keep energy high, explain ideas in simple language, and celebrate effort as well as execution — while still pushing standards.

  • Age-appropriate physical work — fun, safe, and progressive
  • Fundamentals: dribbling, passing, shooting, movement off the ball
  • Small-sided games to practise decisions in tight spaces
  • Support for shy players building confidence; stretch for those ready for more

Small group & pair coaching

Training with a friend or teammates adds realism: communication, competition, and shared learning. We balance individual coaching points with group objectives so every player leaves with something personal to work on.

  • 2v2 / 3v3 and overload practices that mirror match moments
  • Combination play, pressing triggers and transition habits
  • Great value per player compared with 1-to-1, while keeping quality high
  • Popular with siblings, school friends and club teammates

Position-specific coaching

For players who already know their role — wingers, strikers, midfielders, full-backs, centre-backs — we go deeper on positioning, scanning, timing of runs, and the habits scouts and coaches notice.

  • Video-style scenarios on the pitch: “what would you do here?”
  • Profiles tailored to how your club plays (where that helps)
  • Support for trials, academy steps-up, or simply owning a position on match day
